About WEI LAN

Smart waste sorting and recycling equipment manufacturer

Founded in 2019 with RMB 50 million registered capital, Ningbo Wei Lan Environmental Protection Technology Co., Ltd. is a National High-Tech Enterprise focused on environmental technology R&D, intelligent equipment manufacturing, IoT systems, and digital operation platforms for waste sorting and resource recovery.
