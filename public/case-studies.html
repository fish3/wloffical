Buyer Checklist

What serious buyers usually verify in a reference pack

Before moving to technical proposal and quotation, overseas teams typically want to understand whether the reference matches their material, capacity, output target, and operating model.

01

Waste stream similarity

Check whether the reference material mix, contamination level, and preprocessing conditions match the target project.

02

Capacity and footprint

Compare hourly throughput, available site area, and supporting utilities before sizing equipment for local operation.

03

Output expectations

Review the recovered fractions, recycled product direction, and how the system supports value recovery goals.

04

Control and service model

Confirm monitoring scope, operator requirements, commissioning expectations, and long-running support arrangements.
