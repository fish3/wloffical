Product Lines

Two systems for modern resource recovery facilities

Designed for operators who need compact layouts, faster deployment, reliable long-running production, and digital visibility across the complete sorting process.

Super Optical Sorting System

AI optical sorter for waste and recyclable plastics

Modular AI waste sorting machine modules identify and separate PET bottles, HDPE bottles, PP, and mixed plastics by material and color. The automated waste sorting system supports cyclic sorting with intelligent bins and a digital control cabin.

  • 0.6-2 t/h throughput across V1, V2, and V3 configurations
  • 5, 9, or 10 material categories depending on configuration
  • Skid-mounted modules reduce installation and maintenance complexity

View AI optical sorting details

Construction and Bulky Waste Line

Turnkey C&D waste recycling plant and processing line

The Lion One integrated construction waste crusher and screening plant combines feeding, crushing, trommel screening, vibrating screening, magnetic separation, air separation, and baling equipment for recycled aggregate and light materials.

  • 50, 80, 100, 150, or 170 t/h capacity options
  • 35 m2 single-machine footprint for compact project sites
  • Hydraulic drive with adjustable crushing particle size
