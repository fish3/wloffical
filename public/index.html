Product Lines

Two systems for modern resource recovery facilities

Designed for operators who need compact layouts, faster deployment, reliable long-running production, and digital visibility across the complete sorting process.

Super Optical Sorting System

AI optical sorting center for recyclable plastics

Modular sorting modules identify and separate PET bottles, HDPE bottles, PP, and mixed plastics by material and color. The system supports cyclic sorting with intelligent bins and a digital control cabin.

  • 0.6-2 t/h throughput across V1, V2, and V3 configurations
  • 5, 9, or 10 material categories depending on configuration
  • Skid-mounted modules reduce installation and maintenance complexity

View AI optical sorting details

Construction and Bulky Waste Line

Integrated C&D waste disposal and resource utilization line

The Lion One integrated crushing and screening station combines feeding, crushing, screening, magnetic separation, air separation, and baling equipment for recyclable aggregates and light materials.

  • 20-70 t/h capacity range for project sizing
  • 35 m2 single-machine footprint for compact project sites
  • Hydraulic drive with adjustable crushing particle size
