Country and site footprint

Sorted plastic categories, recycled aggregates, metals, light materials, baled recyclable streams, or reporting requirements.

FAQ

Questions that usually come before a quote request

FAQ content supports both buyer conversion and SEO by answering general supplier questions, plastic sorting questions, and construction waste project questions.

General FAQ

What information is needed for a quotation?
Waste type, target capacity, country, site area, project stage, and expected output are usually enough to start a more accurate discussion.
Can WEI LAN customize the system?
System direction can be adjusted around material composition, required purity, footprint, voltage, automation expectations, and output goals.

Plastic Sorting FAQ

Which plastic materials can be evaluated?
Typical materials include transparent PET, green PET, blue PET, mixed-color PET, white HDPE, PP, and mixed recyclable plastics.
What affects sorting configuration?
Material mix, contamination level, color categories, target purity, feed stability, and automation requirements affect the recommended optical sorting setup.

Construction & Bulky Waste FAQ

Which waste streams can be discussed?
Construction waste, decoration waste, demolition waste, bulky waste, mixed sand and aggregate material, wood, plastics, textiles, and metals can be reviewed.
What outputs should be defined early?
Buyers should define recycled aggregates, sand or fines, metals, light materials, baled recyclables, and any local acceptance requirements.
