FAQ

Questions that usually come before a quote request

These are the topics most buyers raise once they start comparing equipment options and preparing internal review materials.

01

What information is needed for a quotation?

Waste type, target capacity, country, site area, and expected output are usually enough to start a more accurate discussion.

02

Can WEI LAN customize the system?

System direction can be adjusted around material composition, required purity, footprint, voltage, and automation expectations.

03

Do you support overseas installation?

Support can include commissioning guidance, operator training, spare parts coordination, and remote follow-up after startup.

04

Can the system integrate with existing equipment?

That depends on process layout and control requirements, but the modular architecture makes integration evaluation more practical.
