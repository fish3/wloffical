Support Modules

A practical delivery chain for recycling projects

The support model is designed to reduce uncertainty for project owners, EPC contractors, and recycling operators who need technical clarity before committing to equipment configuration.

01

Pre-sales consulting

Share waste type, composition, target output, capacity expectation, and local conditions so WEI LAN can recommend a suitable system direction early.

02

Material analysis and process discussion

Photos, videos, contamination level, required purity, target throughput, and output value path help define the practical process route.

03

Layout and configuration planning

Capacity target, available footprint, voltage conditions, and automation level can all be reflected in the proposed layout.

04

Manufacturing and quality control

ISO-backed quality management helps keep production control, equipment assembly, and delivery consistency visible to buyers.

05

Packaging and shipment

Modular equipment structure supports transport planning, container loading strategy, and cleaner handoff for overseas sites.

06

Installation and commissioning

Factory-prefabricated modules can simplify installation sequencing, system startup, and process tuning at project handover.

07

Operator training

Operating guidance can cover equipment controls, routine maintenance, alarms, process adjustments, and safe daily use.

08

Spare parts and maintenance

Recommended spare parts, maintenance routines, inspection points, and replacement planning help reduce downtime after startup.

09

Remote support

Digital monitoring, alarm review, operating data, and remote troubleshooting help local teams keep the line stable after handover.

10

Send Project Details

Overseas buyers can start with material photos or videos, capacity target, site limits, and project information before a full RFQ package is ready.

Support Path

Service stages from early review to after-sales operation

The support path separates early technical review, delivery preparation, and post-startup support so overseas teams can see what information is needed at each stage.

01

Pre-sales

Start the discussion before final specifications are fixed. WEI LAN can review project information and give early direction for system scope.

  • Waste type and composition
  • Material photos or videos
  • Capacity target and output target
  • Project stage and overseas site limits
02

Delivery

Turn the selected equipment path into a practical delivery plan that covers configuration, quality control, shipment, and handover work.

  • Layout and configuration planning
  • Manufacturing and quality control
  • Packaging and shipment coordination
  • Installation and commissioning support
03

After-sales

Support the operating team after startup with training, maintenance planning, spare parts coordination, and remote support.

  • Operator training
  • Spare parts and maintenance
  • Remote support and troubleshooting
  • Operating feedback for process adjustment
